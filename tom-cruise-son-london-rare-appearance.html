Tom Cruise Made a Grand Entrance to London With Son Connor

Tom Cruise just made a super-rare outing with one of his kids (and no, it wasn’t Suri Cruise.)

In case you missed it, Cruise made a super-rare public appearance with his son, Connor, by his side. On June 28, the two arrived at Battersea Power Station’s heliport (Cruise often comes into London by helicopter, for some reason). In photos obtained by Hello, we can see the father-son duo walking through London, getting to their car and meeting fans.

Now, this actually isn’t the most rare thing to see: Cruise and his son Connor together. In 2024 alone, they’ve made headlines for going to public outings together, and Connor being pictured supporting his dad’s acting ventures.
