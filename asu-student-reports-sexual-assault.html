ASU student reports sexual assault WSOC TV

A female ASU student reported to University Police on Monday that she was sexually assaulted by a male acquaintance March 1, according to police.

The student reported that the assault occurred in a residence hall on the west side of campus.

BOONE, N.C. — University Police notified the campus of the assault as required by federal law in an email to campus and an online post Tuesday at http://police.appstate.edu .

Representatives from the police department were not immediately available Wednesday to provide more information.

The department offered these tips on staying safe:
— Be careful when inviting someone into your room or apartment or when going into someone else's room or apartment. This is where most sexual assaults occur.
— Trust your instincts. If you don't feel comfortable in a situation, leave.
— Always be aware of your surroundings. 
— Do not hesitate to call police if you feel you are in an uncomfortable situation.

Anyone with information about any crime is encouraged to call the ASU Police Department at (828) 262-2150 or submit crimes anonymously at http://police.appstate.edu
