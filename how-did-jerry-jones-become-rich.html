How did Jerry Jones become rich?

Jerry Jones is an American businessman who is the owner, president and general manager of the National Football League’s Dallas Cowboys (NFL).

Jerry Jones amassed his wealth through business ventures and investments. Jerry is the founder and owner of Jones Oil and Land Lease, which searches for natural resources such as oil and gas in Arkansas. Jones made his first million dollars in the 1970s through his company.

Jones paid $140 million for the Dallas Cowboys in 1989 after making millions in oil and gas. The Cowboys are now the richest team in the NFL, according to Forbes, with $980 million in revenue and $425 million in operating income in 2020.

Jones formed a joint venture with Yankee Global Enterprises in 2008 to form Legends Hospitality, a food, beverage, merchandise, retail, and stadium operations corporation that serves entertainment venues.

Jones also has stocks in Comstock Resources, real estate, hospitality management, and Papa John’s franchises. As of 2022 Jerry Jones is worth $11.7 billion.
