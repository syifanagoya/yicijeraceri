He played the lead role in Tyler Perry‘s What’s Done in the Dark and Madea Goes to Jail. He was the lead in the Ashanti music video for her track “The Way That I Love You.” He had an early minor role in Diary of a Mad Black Woman in 2005.

Career

He started his career as a model and fitness trainer, as well as acting in stage productions. He made his screen debut in the comedy-drama film Diary of a Crazy Black Woman, starring Kimberly Elise, in which he played a minor role. He has appeared in episodes of Brothers & Sisters, Moonlight, Family Time, Beauty and the Beast, Instant Mom, Mistresses, and Born Again Virgin on television. Keyes had a recurring appearance on the BET comedy series Let’s Stay Together from 2011 to 2014. He also played love interests in Keri Hilson’s music video “Energy,” Ashanti’s “The Way That I Love You,” Ledisi’s “In the Morning,” and Mary J. Blige’s “Suitcase.”
