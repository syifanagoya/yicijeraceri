What is Nicolas Winding Refn's Net Worth?

Nicolas Winding Refn is a Danish film director, screenwriter, and producer who has a net worth of $10 million. Nicolas Winding Refn was born in Copenhagen, Denmark in September 1970. He moved to the United States in 1981 and was raised partly in New York. Refn was expelled from the American Academy of Dramatic Arts for throwing a table into a wall. He wrote, directed, and starred in the 1996 film Pusher and wrote, directed, and produced the 1999 movie Bleeder. Refn wrote and directed the films Fear X in 2003, Pusher II in 2004, and Pusher 3 in 2005.

In 2005 he starred in the movie Kinamand. He wrote and directed the film Bronson in 2008 and directed the movie Agatha Christie's Marple: Nemesis in 2009. Refn wrote and directed the film Valhalla Rising in 2009 and directed the film Drive in 2011. He wrote and directed the movie Only God Forgives in 2013 and starred in the films Jodorowsky's Dune in 2013 and My Life Directed by Nicolas Winding Refn in 2015. In 2016 he wrote, directed, and produced the film The Neon Demon. Refn has won 26 awards and been nominated for 42 others.
