Stefflon Don and Skepta team up for 'Ding-A-Ling' video

It was back in November when we first heard this fire collaboration between Stefflon Don and Skepta, and now we finally have the visuals to feast our eyes on.

This infectious ‘Ding-A-Ling’ samples a scene from The Simpsons and the long-awaited video is a boisterous and high budget affair.

The pair performed the track earlier this week at The VO5 NME Awards, where Stefflon was also awarded Best New Artist.

It’s less than a month until Stefflon Don embarks on her sold out UK tour, and we can’t wait to witness the multitalented performer command the stage.
