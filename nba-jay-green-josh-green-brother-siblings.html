NBA Meet Jay Green, Josh Green Brother

Josh Green brother Jay Green played college basketball for the Northern Arizona Lumberjacks from 2017 to 2021.

In his rookie season, he showcased his skills by achieving an average of 12.1 points, 4.9 rebounds, and 2.1 assists per game.

Renowned for his versatility, Josh Green brother Jay can score effectively from within the paint and beyond the perimeter.

Furthermore, he excels in the defensive and rebounding aspects of the game. While his skills are still developing, Green holds considerable potential to establish himself as a proficient NBA player.

Jay Green Siblings 

Jay Green has three siblings; Josh Green is a well-known basketball player for the Dallas Mavericks of the National Basketball Association (NBA).

His other two siblings are also basketball players. Basketball runs deep within the Green family lineage. His parents have a basketball background as professional players in Australia.

The legacy continues with their children: Josh, Jay, Ky, and Maya. Each of them is emulating their parents’ path, venturing into the realm of basketball and carving out their own distinct identities in the sport’s realm.

Inside Jay Green Parents And Family

Jay Green’s parents are Delmas Green and Cahla Green. Delmas had a college basketball journey at Oregon Tech, while Cahla made her mark as a professional basketball player for the Sydney Flames in Australia.

Both figures have profoundly influenced Jay, instilling valuable life lessons. Delmas Green boasts a history as a former professional basketball player.

His collegiate years were spent at Oregon Tech with the Owls from 1987 to 1991.

During this period, Delmas was honored to be a two-time All-Cascade Conference selection and played an instrumental role in securing the conference championship in 1990.

Similarly, Cahla Green’s story also revolves around professional basketball. She showcased her talents with the Sydney Flames, a prominent presence in the Women’s National Basketball League (WNBL) from 1989 to 1998.

After concluding her basketball journey, Cahla transitioned to a coaching and mentoring role, demonstrating her ongoing dedication to the sport.

As the offspring of these basketball luminaries, Jay and his siblings embrace the path laid out by their parents.
