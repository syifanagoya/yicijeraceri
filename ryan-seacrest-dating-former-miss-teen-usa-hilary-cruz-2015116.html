Ryan Seacrest Dating Former Miss Teen USA Hilary Cruz

Another leading lady! Ryan Seacrest is dating former Miss Teen USA Hilary Cruz, multiple sources confirm to Us Weekly.

A source tells Us that the American Idol host and Cruz, 26, first met at Seacrest’s star-studded 40th birthday bash in Napa, Calif., back in February. The pair got to know each other as they partied alongside Kim and Khloe Kardashian, Randy Jackson, and Paula Abdul.
