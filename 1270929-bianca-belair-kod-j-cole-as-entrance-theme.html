Bianca Belair Loves Her Current Theme Song, But Wouldnt Mind Using J. Coles K.O.D. In Th

Bianca Belair has one of the most notable theme songs in WWE, however the ‘EST of WWE’ has the perfect song in mind if she were to ever switch it up.

Former SmackDown Women’s Champion Bianca Belair spoke with the Milwaukee Record and talked about her current theme song, as well as meeting Megan Thee Stallion recently. Asked if there’s any other tracks out there that she’d entertain using in WWE, Belair said she really loves the one she has, but J. Cole has a song that would be very fitting for her wrestling persona.

“I really love my theme music. I think it fits me.” Belair told the Milwaukee Record. “I think that whenever my theme music plays, you can’t help but bounce around and move around, and I think it’s really just contagious. You know, with the crowd and the crowd feels it. You see the crowd bopping around and their hands bouncing around. So, I don’t think I would change my theme music ever. I’m a big fan of Meg’s music. I’m a big fan of J. Cole’s music. He even has a song called “K.O.D.” that’s the same name as my finisher: KOD, the Kiss of Death, so I think that might tie in very well in the future, but I really do love my theme music.”

Belair also spoke about having great ring gear to compliment her entrance theme, and was asked if she has people hitting her up to make gear for them too.

“I take a lot of pride in making my own gear. It’s just an amazing feeling to go out there and stand in the ring and have these big, amazing moments in the ring gear that I made. Or I look at a video game and see that it’s the gear that I made or an action figure. I try to make gear where I only wear it one time for big, major events so every single gear has a significance to it.

“But yeah, sometimes maybe we’re in the locker room and a girl might have a tear in her gear and I’m in there sewing the gear up, helping her with the tear. My husband, I do some of his stuff every now and then, but for the most part, I’m so busy with my stuff. I kind of put myself in a hole with building a reputation of every time someone sees me I kind of have a different gear on. So I’m always busy trying to make new gear so I can have new gear for Raw.”
