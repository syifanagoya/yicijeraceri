What is Jo Marie Payton's Net Worth?

Jo Marie Payton is an American actress and singer who has a net worth of $4 million. Jo Marie Payton was born in Albany, Georgia in August 1950. She is best known for starring as Harriette Winslow in the TV series Family Matters and Perfect Strangers. Payton had recurring roles on the TV series The New Odd Couple and Silver Spoons. From 1987 to 1989 she starred as Harriette Winslow on the television series Perfect Strangers. She reprised that role from 1989 to 1997 in the series Family Matters. Payton had recurring roles in the TV series Moesha and Will & Grace and then voiced the role of Suga Mama in the series The Proud Family from 2001 to 2005. She had a recurring role in the TV series The Rev in 2013 and Mann and Wife from 2015 to 2016. Payton has been nominated for three Image Awards and three NAMIC Vision Awards. In 2005 she co-hosted the 15th Annual NAACP Theatre Awards.
